AMOC

The Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation (AMOC) is a crucial component of the Earth's climate system. It plays a key role in redistributing heat, freshwater, oxygen, carbon, and nutrients on a global scale. Therefore, the AMOC is vital for maintaining the global climate and weather patterns, as well as marine ecosystems. Our lab focuses on understanding the dynamics of the AMOC and its impact on climate variability and change using a combination of observational and numerical modeling data.
